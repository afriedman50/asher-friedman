About me

Hey — I'm Asher, a Mechanical Engineering student at Georgia Tech with a minor in Industrial Design. I'm in my third year and constantly fascinated by the overlap between how things work and how they feel to use.

I approach every project with an engineer's rigor and a designer's eye. Whether I'm running stress analysis on a structural component or sketching out a product from scratch, I'm always thinking about the person on the other end of it.

When I'm not in the lab or studio, I play guitar and piano, make visual art, and find ways to keep building. I pull inspiration from all of it — creativity in engineering comes from everywhere.
